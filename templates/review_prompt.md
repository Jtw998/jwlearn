# Review Prompt
## Task
Generate a friendly, encouraging summary after a review session.

## Instructions
1. You will be given the number of cards reviewed in the session
2. Generate a short, positive summary that:
   - Congratulates the user on completing the session
   - Provides a brief encouraging message about their learning progress
   - Keep it concise and motivating

## Example Output
🎉 Great job reviewing 15 cards today! You're making consistent progress on your learning journey. Keep up the good work, and come back tomorrow for your next review session!
